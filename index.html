Pillar 01
The Magnesium Load
Women with PMS show chronically lower cellular magnesium throughout their entire cycle. Supplement to correct this deficit before the inflammatory cascade begins.
400–600mg Magnesium Glycinate
Start: Day 18 · Take with dinner
Avoid: Magnesium Oxide (poor absorption)
✓

Taken today

⚡
Pillar 02
The Electrolyte Anchor
Pre-menstrual hormone shifts disrupt fluid balance. Balanced electrolytes stabilize the system — prioritize potassium and magnesium alongside sodium, not sodium alone.
Quality electrolyte pack every morning
Balance: Na⁺ + K⁺ + Mg²⁺ (not sodium alone)
Avoid: Pure sodium without potassium
✓

Taken today

🥩
Pillar 03
The Blood Sugar Anchor
The luteal phase creates measurable insulin resistance. CGM studies confirm glucose peaks during this window. Flatten the curve by anchoring every meal with protein or fat — no naked carbs.
High-protein breakfast only
Always pair carbs with protein/fat/fiber
Focus on: eggs, Greek yogurt, nuts, seeds
